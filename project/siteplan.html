Skill Empowerment

Skill Empowerment Site is a project designed to provide users with access to resources, tutorials, and guidance on developing practical and professional skills. The site aims to empower individuals to enhance their skill sets and achieve personal and career growth.

Optional domain availability: www.skillempowerment.org

Skill Empowerment Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with access to resources, tutorials, and guidance on developing practical and professional skills. It aims to empower individuals to enhance their skill sets and achieve personal and career growth.

Scenarios

  • Are the empowerment skills free?
  • How do I become a member or get involved with the training?
  • What types of skills are offered on the site?
  • Can I access the resources and tutorials on mobile devices?
  • Is there a community or forum for users to share experiences and tips?
  • How often are new resources and tutorials added to the site?
  • Are there any prerequisites for accessing the skill development resources?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color - Forest Green (#2e7d32): Used for headings, footer, and navigation bar background.
  • Secondary Color - Sand Beige (#f5f5dc): Used for section background and nav links .

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat – used for all major headings and titles.
  • Body Font: Roboto – used for paragraphs and list items for readability.
